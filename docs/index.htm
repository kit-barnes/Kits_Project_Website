ABOUT THIS SITE

Things I've spent my time on
(other than family, work, chores, skiing, science fiction, photography, video games... )

I took my MSEE to work for the old Bell System and spent my time mostly designing and coding embedded systems. While I've been retired for a while now, I still enjoy getting something working the way I think it should. Admittedly, the way I think something should work occasionally has a tendency to squirm and mutate as I find myself banging my head against some wall

Kit Barnes


Welcome to my website. The projects below are a sampling of the things I've been playing with for the last 50 years. Some of them will link to their own repositories. Others are only documented by pages on this site. Few of them I really consider complete. All of them have helped stretch my brain a bit. I'm publishing them here with the hope that some portion may be useful.
